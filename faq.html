Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated:

1. How do I download lyrics using this app?

  1. Either:
    • Drop an audio file into the app or browse to select one, or
    • Manually enter the artist name, track title, and (optionally) album name.
  2. Check the box to prefer synced lyrics, if desired.
  3. Click Download Lyrics. The lyrics will appear below if found.

Tip: Accurate metadata or manual input improves the chances of finding the correct lyrics.

2. What file formats are supported?

Most common audio formats are supported, including MP3, FLAC, WAV, M4A, AAC, and others.

3. Is this app free to use?

Yes! Qlyrics Downloader is completely free. No accounts, subscriptions, or ads. Donations are welcome but never required.

4. Do I need an internet connection?

Yes. While the app runs locally and doesn’t upload your files, it does connect to lyric sources online to fetch lyrics.

5. Do I need an account or login?

No. We don’t require accounts, logins, or any personal data.

6. Is it safe and private?

Absolutely. We don’t use cookies, trackers, or analytics. No data is collected or stored. Your audio files never leave your device.

7. What’s the difference between synced and unsynced lyrics?

Synced lyrics include timestamps and align with playback — ideal for karaoke or media players. Unsynced lyrics are plain text without timing.

8. Why didn’t lyrics appear for my file or search?

Possible reasons:

  • Missing or inaccurate song metadata
  • Manual input had typos or incomplete info
  • Lyrics not available from supported sources
  • Temporary connection or service issue

Try editing the metadata, correcting manual input, or using a different version of the song.

9. Is it legal to download lyrics?

The app is intended for personal and lawful use — such as music study, karaoke, or accessibility. You are responsible for how you use it and must comply with copyright laws and terms of service.

10. Can I upload files to check for lyrics?

No uploads occur. All processing is done locally on your device. The app only uses song metadata — whether extracted from your audio file or manually entered — to search for lyrics online.

11. How can I support the project?

If you find the app helpful, consider making a donation via our donation page. Your support helps keep the app free and open for everyone.

12. Who can I contact for feedback or help?

Please visit our Contact page for questions, feedback, or support. We appreciate hearing from you!
