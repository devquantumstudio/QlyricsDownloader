About This Project

Last updated:

Qlyrics Downloader is a free, lightweight application designed to fetch synced or unsynced lyrics in LRC format from supported sources. It’s built to be fast, clean, and private — no accounts, no ads, and no trackers.

What We Believe

  • Free & non-monetized: You can use the app without paying. Donations are optional and never required.
  • Privacy first: We don’t collect data, use cookies, or integrate with analytics platforms.
  • Minimal footprint: Lightweight interface, no bloat, no pop-ups, no distractions.

How It Works

  1. Drop an audio file or browse to select one.
  2. Check the box to prefer synced LRC (if available).
  3. Click Download Lyrics. The lyrics will appear below with timestamps (if synced).

Tip: For best results, ensure you provide accurate song metadata.

What We Don’t Do

  • No ads, no cookies, no tracking scripts.
  • No user accounts and no personal data collection.
  • No uploading or storing of your audio files.

Respectful & Lawful Use

This app is intended for users who wish to download lyrics for personal or lawful use, such as music study, karaoke, or accessibility purposes. You are responsible for how you use the app and must comply with applicable laws and terms of service.

Prohibited: using the app to distribute copyrighted lyrics without permission, scraping restricted content, or violating any intellectual property laws.

Donations

This is a community-driven project provided free of charge. If you find it helpful, feel free to support us with a voluntary donation via our donation page. Donations are voluntary gifts, not payments for a service.

App & Tech

  • Runs as a standalone app — no browser required.
  • Fully self-contained: no plugins, extensions, or add-ons needed.
  • Cross-platform: available for Windows and Android.
  • Privacy-respecting: no accounts, analytics, or third-party ads.
