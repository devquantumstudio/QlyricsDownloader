Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Qlyrics Downloader is a free, privacy-respecting app. We do not monetize, we do not serve ads, and we do not use cookies or trackers. This page explains what data is — and isn’t — processed when you use the app.

1) Quick Summary

  • No accounts and no signup required.
  • No cookies and no third-party cookies.
  • No ads, no trackers, no analytics.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data. Ever.
  • Donations are optional; payment processing is handled externally (e.g., Ko-fi). We do not see your payment details.

2) Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, IP address, or location. We do not use analytics services, and we do not track your activity across the app or the web.

3) What Happens When You Use the App

When you load an audio file or manually enter metadata (like artist name and track title), the app uses that information solely to search for matching lyrics from supported sources. Your audio file is never uploaded or transmitted — all processing happens locally on your device.

Technical note: The app may contact public lyric sources using metadata you provide. In doing so, those services may receive standard technical details (such as your IP address and user-agent) in order to respond to the request. We do not add any identifiers, and we do not store or transmit your audio files.

4) Local Runtime & Logs

The app runs fully on your device. We do not operate central servers to handle your activity, and we do not log any of your usage. If the app contacts third-party lyric sources, those services may generate their own logs under their own privacy policies — we do not receive or access those logs.

5) Cookies & Local Storage

  • Cookies: We do not set cookies.
  • Third-party cookies: Not used.
  • Local/session storage: Not used to store personal data.
  • Push notifications / service workers: Not used.

6) Donations

Donations are completely optional and are handled by third-party platforms (e.g., Ko-fi). These platforms may collect information needed to process your donation, including payment details, under their own privacy policies. We do not receive your card or billing information. If you choose to share a message, name, or amount, we may see that limited information.

7) Third-Party Lyric Sources

To fetch lyrics, the app may query publicly available third-party lyric sources using the metadata you provide (e.g., artist name, track title). These requests are made directly from your device and are not routed through our servers.

We do not control these external sources, and we are not responsible for their accuracy, availability, or their privacy practices. Your interaction with them is limited to read-only search requests based on metadata. No personal information is shared with them by this app.

8) Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Your Choices

  • You can use the app completely anonymously — no login, cookies, or tracking.
  • You can choose not to donate; the app is fully free.
  • You may contact us if you have questions about this privacy policy.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes. Continued use of the app after an update implies acceptance of the revised policy.

11) Contact

If you have privacy questions or concerns, please reach out via our Contact page.
